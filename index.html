About me
Mechanical engineer | Sustainable energy engineer | Building simulation engineer | Machine learning

I am a Mechanical Engineer with a Master in Sustainable Energy Technology from Eindhoven University of Technology. With a specialization in Building Performance Simulation (BPS), I worked in the building sector for about 4 years, developing design strategies for energy performance, natural ventilation, indoor comfort and fire safety. Strategized developed were for both building systems/installations and building design located in Netherlands, Ireland, Dubai, Qatar and India. I am currently working as a PhD researcher in the department of Architectural Engineering in KU Leuven, researching on a Machine Learning Method suitable for design stage application to predict building energy.
